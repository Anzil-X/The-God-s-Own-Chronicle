Amusement Parks

Wonderla Kochi

Wonderla Kochi is one of the largest amusement parks in India, offering a wide range of thrilling rides and attractions. From high-speed roller coasters to water slides, it caters to adventure lovers of all ages. The park also features a variety of food options and entertainment shows, making it a perfect family outing destination.
Location: Wonderla Amusement Park, Kochi, Kerala, India.
Address: 28th km, Kochi-Munnar Road, Pallikara, Kochi, Kerala 683557. View on Google Maps

Fantasy Park

Fantasy Park, located in Malampuzha, is a family-friendly amusement park known for its beautiful gardens and exciting rides. The park offers a mix of thrilling rides and gentle attractions suitable for younger children. It is a popular spot for families looking to enjoy a day of fun amidst nature.
Location: Fantasy Park, Malampuzha, Kerala, India.
Address: Malampuzha, Palakkad, Kerala 678651. View on Google Maps

Silver Storm

Silver Storm is a popular amusement park located in Athirapally, Thrissur, featuring a variety of water rides and amusement attractions. It is ideal for those looking to cool off while enjoying thrilling water slides and activities. The park provides an exhilarating experience for visitors of all ages, making it a must-visit for fun seekers.
Location: Silver Storm Water Theme Park, Athirapally, Thrissur, Kerala, India.
Address: Silver Storm Water Theme Park, Athirapally, Thrissur, Kerala 680724. View on Google Maps
