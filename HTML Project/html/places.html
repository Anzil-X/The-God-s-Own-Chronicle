Tourist Places

Alleppey

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is a stunning destination in Kerala famous for its intricate network of backwaters. The serene landscapes are dotted with traditional houseboats, offering visitors a chance to explore the lush green surroundings and local villages. Experience the vibrant culture through local cuisine and festivals. The picturesque views and calm waters make it an ideal spot for photography and relaxation.
Location: Kerala, India. View on Google Maps

Munnar

Nestled in the Western Ghats, Munnar is a breathtaking hill station renowned for its sprawling tea gardens, cool climate, and scenic landscapes. Visitors can hike through the hills, explore the fragrant tea plantations, and enjoy the panoramic views of the valleys. Munnar is also home to unique flora and fauna, including the endangered Nilgiri Tahr at the Eravikulam National Park. Whether you seek adventure or tranquility, Munnar offers a perfect escape.
Location: Kerala, India. View on Google Maps

Fort Kochi

Fort Kochi is a historic area known for its rich colonial heritage, charming streets, and vibrant culture. The area is famous for its Chinese fishing nets, St. Francis Church, and the Indo-Portuguese Museum. Visitors can enjoy leisurely walks along the beach, savor local seafood, and experience the lively arts scene. Fort Kochi is also a hub for traditional performances like Kathakali and Kalaripayattu, making it a unique cultural experience.
Location: Kerala, India. View on Google Maps
