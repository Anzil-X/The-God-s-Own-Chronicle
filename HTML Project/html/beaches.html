Beaches

Varkala Beach

Varkala Beach, known for its stunning cliffs and golden sands, is a picturesque destination that feels like a painting brought to life. The beach is famous for its mineral springs, which are believed to have healing properties, attracting both tourists and pilgrims alike. It's an ideal spot for relaxation, beach sports, and enjoying vibrant sunset views.
Location: Varkala, Kerala, India.
Address: Varkala, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala 695141. View on Google Maps

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam Beach is like a vibrant festival of sun, sand, and sea, where every wave tells a story. Famous for its crescent-shaped coastline and lighthouses, it offers a mix of relaxation and adventure. Whether you're sunbathing, swimming, or indulging in water sports, Kovalam promises a memorable experience for everyone.
Location: Kovalam, Kerala, India.
Address: Kovalam Beach, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala 695527. View on Google Maps

Cherai Beach

Cherai Beach is a tranquil haven, reminiscent of a peaceful painting where the sea kisses the shore. Located near Kochi, this beach is known for its clean waters and the unique experience of watching dolphins play in the waves. It's perfect for leisurely strolls and enjoying the serene beauty of Kerala's coastline.
Location: Cherai, Kerala, India.
Address: Cherai Beach, Ernakulam, Kerala 683514. View on Google Maps
